Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tarai Organics Farm Made Cultured Ghee?

Tarai Organics Farm Made Cultured Ghee is premium ghee crafted from the A2 milk of Desi Sahiwal cows at Tarai Organic Farms, using the traditional Bilona method. It is known for its rich flavour and health benefits.

What makes Tarai Organics Cultured Ghee different from regular ghee?

Our ghee is made using the Bilona method at Tarai Organic Farms, which involves churning curd obtained from A2 milk to produce fresh cultured butter. This process retains natural nutrients and enhances flavour.

Why is A2 milk important in making this ghee?

A2 milk from Desi Sahiwal cows at Tarai Organic Farms contains A2 beta-casein protein, which is considered easier to digest and beneficial for overall health compared to A1 protein found in some cow breeds.

How is the Bilona method beneficial?

The Bilona method practiced at Tarai Organic Farms preserves the natural properties of milk and butter, including vitamins, antioxidants, and essential fatty acids like DHA and CLA. It results in ghee that supports immunity, digestion, and overall well-being.

Can Tarai Organics Cultured Ghee be consumed by lactose-intolerant individuals?

Yes, the A2 protein in our ghee sourced from Tarai Organic Farms is easier to digest and may be suitable for lactose-intolerant individuals. It contains proline, which aids in digestion.
